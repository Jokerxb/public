How to protect yourself when going to public places?

a.Minimize activities in crowded public places. If it is necessary to go, try to carry a disposable medical mask with you, and wear it when the ventilation is poor or the distance between you and others is less than 1m.

b.When coughing or sneezing, completely cover the mouth and nose with paper towel or elbow sleeve; Throw the used paper towels into the closed dustbin; If you cover your hands when coughing or sneezing, wash your hands with running water and soap, or scrub your hands with alcohol-free disinfectant.

c.Carry disinfectant wipes with you. Wash your hands or wipe with disinfectant wipes in time after contacting public goods or public facilities to avoid direct contact with your mouth, eyes and nose.

d.Wash hands properly after going out and going home to ensure hand hygiene and avoid hand transmission

What are the precautions for shopping in supermarkets and shopping malls?

a.Before shopping, make a list and minimize your stay.

b.Try to avoid the peak passenger flow in shopping malls and supermarkets and reduce the opportunity to contact others.

c.Before entering supermarkets and shopping malls, cooperate with temperature detection, "Health Code" registration, etc

d.Wear masks correctly throughout the process, and keep more than 1m away from others when shopping and checking out.

e.When taking the elevator, the escalator is preferred; If you must take a van elevator, wear a mask.

f.During checkout, the non-contact code scanning method is preferred.

g.Wash your hands immediately after you go home.

What are the precautions for dining out?

a.Try to reduce multi person meals..

b.Choose a restaurant with good sanitary conditions and eat off peak as much as possible to avoid crowding. Crowded.

c.Before entering the restaurant, cooperate with the staff to measure the body temperature and obey the management of the restaurant staff.

d.During dining, minimize access to public facilities. If you need to queue up for meals, pay attention to keep a distance of 1m.

e.Try to order and pay by non-contact means such as online or code scanning.
